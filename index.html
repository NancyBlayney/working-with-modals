Name:
Nancy

About:

Nancy loves all things ocean! If she had her way, she would be a mermaid and spend her time swimming all over the world, from the kelp forests of Southern California to the Great Barrier Reef off the eastern coast of Australia.

She draws her inspiration for her photography from life around her. The colors and sounds and smells of the world around her paint a vibrant picture on the canvas of life, and she tries to capture that feeling of exuberance in her photos.

Please enjoy the gallery!
